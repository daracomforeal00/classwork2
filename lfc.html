Historical Bacground of the District

The District was being cordinated by Pastor Isaiah far back as 2009 hosted other zones like Idiaba, Momodu House and Baba Ode. His presence as the District Cordinator brought about unity among members and a slight increase in the membership were recorded. Tenagers were encouraged to become cell menisters where there are no cell ministers available. After a short period of time, He handed over to Pastor Momodu of blessed memories.

In 2012, Pastor Momodu took over the leadership of the District as the District Pastor, He was tagged a "Rogged and Rural Pastor" very agile and willing to ensure that there is replication in most rural area of the District, which brought about the creation of 75 Acres Zone at Nahco area and Baba Ode Zone and was also created. He Handed over to his Assistant the person of Pastor Udoh Sunday in 2014

Pastor Sunday who seems to be a stylish and vibrant Pastor also ensure the work of God increased and multiplication of members were recorded. He served as District Pastor a period of 2year and handed over to Pastor Olaleye who began his tenure as in 2017 till date.
